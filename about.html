About Tikaaram

An educational enterprise built for inclusive and accessible learning

Our Mission

Tikaaram is an educational enterprise focused on helping children learn step by step through affordable and easy-to-use learning materials. It aims to strengthen foundational skills in literacy and numeracy by making learning more structured, guided, and accessible.

Why This Matters

Many Filipino learners face barriers beyond the classroom. Poverty, limited academic support, and lack of learning materials affect their ability to fully develop foundational skills.

15.5% of Filipinos live below the poverty line (PSA, 2023)
Up to 25% affected using international poverty standards (World Bank)
90% learning poverty among 10-year-olds (EDCOM II, 2023)
2.9M out-of-school children and youth due to poverty-related barriers

Key Insight

Data shows that many children are not failing because they are incapable, but because foundational learning is not being effectively built early. School systems often assume learners can focus solely on academics, but for many students, education competes with poverty, hunger, and lack of support.

What Tikaaram Provides

Step-by-step structured learning materials
Affordable and accessible educational resources
Instant feedback through OMR scanning system
Performance tracking for continuous improvement
Data-informed learning support system

Project ARAL-UGAT

Project ARAL-UGAT is Tikaaram’s advocacy arm that provides tutoring and learning support to underserved learners. It aims to bridge learning gaps by offering guidance beyond traditional classroom instruction, especially for students affected by poverty and limited resources.
